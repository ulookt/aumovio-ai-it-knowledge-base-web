1) PASSWORD RESET (SELF-SERVICE)

Keywords: password, reset, credentials, account, portal, sspr

Summary:
This article explains how employees can reset their Aumovio company password using the Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) portal without contacting IT support.

Instructions:
1. Open the Aumovio Self-Service Password Reset page:
   https://sspr.aumovio.ai
2. Enter your company email address or UPN (example: firstname.lastname@aumovio.ai).
3. Select a verification method and complete identity verification:
   - SMS verification code
   - Microsoft Authenticator app
4. Create a new password that meets the Aumovio security policy:
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter
   - At least one lowercase letter
   - At least one number
   - At least one special character
5. Submit the password reset request.
6. Wait 2 to 3 minutes for the password to synchronize across systems.
7. Sign in again to Windows, Outlook, Microsoft Teams, and other company services.

Troubleshooting:
1. If no verification methods are available, contact IT support to register a phone number or authenticator app.
2. If the account is locked, wait 15 minutes and try again.
3. If the problem continues, contact IT support.

Notes:
1. Password changes may take several minutes to propagate to all company systems.

2) VPN SETUP AND CONNECTION (WINDOWS AND MACOS)

Keywords: vpn, remote access, client, connection, certificate, split tunnel

Summary:
This article describes how to install and connect to the Aumovio corporate VPN for secure remote access to internal systems.

Instructions (Windows):
1. Download the approved VPN client from the Aumovio IT portal:
   https://it-portal.aumovio.ai/software
2. Install the VPN client on your device.
3. Import the VPN configuration profile provided by IT:
   aumovio-corporate-vpn.conf
4. Launch the VPN client.
5. Sign in using your Aumovio company credentials.
6. Approve the multi-factor authentication (MFA) request.
7. Confirm the VPN connection by accessing internal resources such as:
   - https://intranet.aumovio.ai
   - Company network drives

Instructions (macOS):
1. Download and install the approved VPN client from the IT portal:
   https://it-portal.aumovio.ai/software
2. Import the VPN configuration profile.
3. Launch the VPN client and sign in with company credentials.
4. Approve the MFA prompt.
5. Verify access to internal company resources.

Troubleshooting:
1. Restart the VPN client and try again.
2. Ensure your internet connection is stable.
3. Confirm that the MFA request was approved.
4. Restart your device if the connection fails repeatedly.
5. Contact IT support if the VPN still does not connect.

Notes:
1. When connected to the VPN, some internet traffic may be routed through the company network (split tunneling).

3) CONTACTING IT SUPPORT

Keywords: it support, helpdesk, contact, ticket

Summary:
Use this process if you are unable to resolve an issue using the self-service guides.

Instructions:
1. Open the Aumovio IT Support Portal:
   https://support.aumovio.ai
2. Create a new support ticket.
3. Provide a clear and detailed description of the issue.
4. Include screenshots, error messages, or log files if available.
5. Submit the ticket and wait for confirmation.

Notes:
1. High-priority incidents may be escalated automatically.
2. Response times depend on issue severity and business impact.
3. You can track ticket status through the support portal.
