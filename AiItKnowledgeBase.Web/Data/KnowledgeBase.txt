1) Password Reset (Self-Service)

Keywords: password, reset, credentials, account, portal, sspr

Summary:
This article explains how employees can reset their company password using the self-service password reset (SSPR) portal.

Instructions:

1. Open the company Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) page: `<company-sspr-url>`.
2. Enter your company email address or UPN.
3. Complete the identity verification process (for example, SMS code or authenticator app).
4. Choose a new password that meets the company policy:

   Minimum 12 characters
   Combination of uppercase and lowercase letters
   At least one number and one symbol
5. Wait 2â€“3 minutes for the password to synchronize.
6. Sign in again to Windows, Outlook, or Microsoft Teams.

Troubleshooting:

If no verification methods are available, contact IT support to register a phone number or authenticator app.

Notes:

Password changes may take several minutes to propagate to all company services.

2) VPN Setup and Connection (Windows & macOS)

Keywords: vpn, remote access, client, connection, certificate, split tunnel

Summary:
This article describes how to install and connect to the corporate VPN for remote access.

Instructions (Windows):

1. Download and install the approved VPN client from the IT portal: `<it-portal-url>`.
2. Launch the VPN client and import the provided configuration profile: `<vpn-config-file>`.
3. Sign in using your company credentials.
4. Approve the multi-factor authentication (MFA) request.
5. Confirm the connection by accessing internal websites or network drives.

Instructions (macOS):

1. Download and install the approved VPN client from the IT portal.
2. Import the VPN configuration profile.
3. Sign in with your company credentials and approve the MFA prompt.
4. Verify that internal resources are accessible.

Troubleshooting:

Restart the VPN client and try again.
Ensure your internet connection is active.
Verify that MFA approval was completed successfully.
If the VPN still does not connect, restart your device.
Contact IT support if the problem persists.

Notes:

When connected to the VPN, some internet traffic may be routed through the company network (split tunneling).

3) Contacting IT Support

Keywords: it support, helpdesk, contact, ticket

Summary:
Use this process if you cannot resolve an issue using the self-service guides.

Instructions:

1. Create a ticket in the IT support portal: `<it-support-url>`.
2. Provide a clear description of the issue.
3. Include screenshots or error messages if available.
4. Submit the ticket and wait for confirmation.

Notes:

High-priority incidents may be escalated automatically.
Response times depend on issue severity.
